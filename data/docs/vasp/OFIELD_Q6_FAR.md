# OFIELD_Q6_FAR

Categories: INCAR tag, Advanced molecular-dynamics sampling

OFIELD\_Q6\_FAR = [real]

Description: OFIELD\_Q6\_FAR sets the far fading distance for the computation of a continuous $Q\_6$ parameter in the interface-pinning method.

---

The unit of OFIELD\_Q6\_FAR is $\AA$.
