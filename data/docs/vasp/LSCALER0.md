# LSCALER0

Categories: INCAR tag, Exchange-correlation functionals, Van der Waals functionals

LSCALER0 = .TRUE. | .FALSE.  
 Default: **LSCALER0** = .TRUE.

Description: LSCALER0  decides whether to re-scale the van der Waals radii $R\_{0}$ (LSCALER0=*.TRUE.*) or not.
