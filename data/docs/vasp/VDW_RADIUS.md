# VDW_RADIUS

Categories: INCAR tag, Exchange-correlation functionals, Van der Waals functionals

VDW\_RADIUS = [real]  
 Default: **VDW\_RADIUS** = 50

Description: Defines the cutoff radius (in $\AA$) for the van der Waals pair interactions.

---

VDW\_RADIUS sets the cutoff radius for the van der Waals pair interactions in $\AA$ for the DFT-D2, DFT-D3, DFT-ulg, DDsC, and Tkatchenko-Scheffler methods.
