# LVDW_EWALD

Categories: INCAR tag, Exchange-correlation functionals, Van der Waals functionals

LVDW\_EWALD = logical  
 Default: **LVDW\_EWALD** = .FALSE.

Description: LVDW\_EWALD decides whether the lattice summation in $E\_{disp}$ expression by means of Ewald's summation is computed.

---

LVDW\_EWALD decides whether the lattice summation in $E\_{disp}$ expression by means of Ewald's summation is computed in the pairwise van der Waals corrections schemes DFT-D2, DFT-ulg and Tkatchenko-Scheffler method.
