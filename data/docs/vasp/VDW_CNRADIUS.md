# VDW_CNRADIUS

Categories: INCAR tag, Exchange-correlation functionals, Van der Waals functionals

VDW\_CNRADIUS = [real]  
 Default: **VDW\_CNRADIUS** = 21.167

Description: VDW\_CNRADIUS defines the cutoff radius (in $\AA$) for the calculation of the coordination numbers.

---

VDW\_CNRADIUS sets the cutoff radius (in $\AA$) for the calculation of the coordination numbers used for the DFT-D3 method.
