# IRC_DELTA0

Categories: INCAR tag, Transition states

IRC\_DELTA0 = real  
 Default: **IRC\_DELTA0** = 0.0015

Description: Defines the tolerance factor $\Delta\_0$ in Å used in the IRC calculations.

---

The smaller the value of IRC\_DELTA0, the closer the computed IRC trajectory follows the true IRC pathway. However, a small tolerance factor $\Delta\_0$ necessitates more time steps and, thus, computational effort.
